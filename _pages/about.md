I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Colorado Boulder, co-advised by Drs. [Yueqi Chen](http://cusecurity.cs.colorado.edu/yueqichen/) and [Ashutosh Trivedi](https://ashutoshtrivedi.com/). My work sits at the intersection of systems and security. I focus on building reliable and scalable systems, and I have been exploring how machine learning can help with program analysis and system design.

Outside of research, I spend a lot of time behind a camera. I am drawn to dramatic light, changing weather, and quiet moments in landscapes and wildlife. You can view some of my work [here](https://photography.sriranga.me).
